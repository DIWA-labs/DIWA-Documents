## 1. Purpose and Scope

This document summarizes categories of use that typically require a Commercial License under the DIWA licensing framework.

This policy is provided for informational purposes only. The governing terms for any specific use remain the applicable DIWA license, agreement, or written authorization issued by DIWA Research Labs.

In the event of any conflict between this policy and an applicable DIWA license or agreement, the license or agreement controls.

If you are uncertain whether your use requires a Commercial License, contact DIWA Research Labs before proceeding.

## 2. Commercial Use Threshold

Under the DIWA licensing framework, the Commercial Use Threshold is annual gross revenue exceeding USD $1,000,000.

Organizations at or below this threshold may still require a Commercial License depending on their funding structure, organizational type, or intended use.

Refer to the applicable DIWA license for controlling eligibility requirements.

## 3. Commercial License Generally Required

The following categories of use generally require a Commercial License under the applicable DIWA licenses and agreements:
• Universities, colleges, research institutions, and laboratories conducting sponsored, grant-funded, contract-funded, or commercially funded research or services.
• Government agencies, government-funded programs, and entities deriving more than thirty percent (30%) of annual revenue from government contracts.
• Consulting firms and professional service organizations providing services to third parties for compensation.
• For-profit corporations and entities that have received aggregate external investment, venture funding, or grants exceeding USD $2,000,000.
• Organizations exceeding the Commercial Use Threshold.
• Commercial hosting, enterprise deployment, or institutional use not otherwise expressly permitted under an applicable DIWA license.
• Redistribution, sublicensing, resale, commercialization, or commercial exploitation of the DIWA Engine Core or Derivative Engine Works.

## 4. Contact Required to Determine License Need

The following activities may require a Commercial License depending on scope, deployment model, and business arrangement:
• Proprietary enterprise integrations incorporating DIWA technologies.
• White-label deployments of products or services built using DIWA technologies.
• Commercial distribution of modified DIWA engine technologies or Derivative Engine Works.
• Custom licensing arrangements not covered by existing DIWA licenses.
• Any use that does not clearly fall within an existing free-use category.

Users should obtain written confirmation from DIWA Research Labs before proceeding with any such activity.

Proceeding without confirmation may result in a violation of the applicable DIWA license.

## 5. Independent Developers and Studios

Independent developers, creators, and studios that qualify for free use under the DIWA Applications and Game Layer Terms generally do not require a Commercial License.

Users should consult the DIWA Applications and Game Layer Terms for the controlling eligibility requirements, restrictions, and permitted uses.

## 6. How to Obtain a Commercial License

Commercial licenses are issued directly by DIWA Research Labs.

Requests should be submitted to:
diwa.research.labs@google.com

Please include:
• Organization name and organizational type.
• Description of intended use.
• Expected scale of deployment or distribution.
• Relevant funding, investment, or organizational context.

DIWA Research Labs will review the request and provide applicable licensing terms if appropriate.

A Commercial License is not effective unless confirmed in writing by an authorized representative of DIWA Research Labs.
